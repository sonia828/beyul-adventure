EXPLORE NEPAL

Nepal is a Himalayan Kingdom blessed with amazing cultural and landscape diversity making it one of

the most exciting destinations for adventure enthusiasts.

Khumbu
The Gateway of Everest
The Khumbu region, also known as the Everest region, is home to the Sherpa people and is arguably the most famous mountain region in Nepal if not the world.
Langtang
The Land of Tamang people
The Langtang region is located north of Kathmandu and borders the Tibet Region. The majority of people residing in the Langtang region are of Tamang heritage.
Manaslu
The Forbidden Kingdom
Manaslu, locally known as Kutung, is the eighth highest mountain in the world at 8,156 metres (26,759 ft).
Kanchenjunga
The Five Treasures of Snows
Until 1852, Kanchenjunga was assumed to be the highest mountain in the world until George Everest named Everest as the highest peak.
Mustang
The Lost Tibetian Empire
Mustang is a remote, semi-independent Tibetan kingdom within Nepal. The culture is Tibetan Buddhist and the people are either Thakalis.
Dolpa
The Last Trading Post of the Trans-Himalayan Route
Dolpa is a high-altitude Tibetan cultural region in the upper part of the Dolpa district of western Nepal.
